Satay

Description

Southeast Asian dish of seasoned, skewered and grilled meat, served with a sauce

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 16 ounce jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta for 8 to 10 minutes
  2. Preheat oven to 350F then simmer spaghetti for 5 minutes
  3. Combine mozzarella, Cheddar and Ricotta
  4. Bake in preheated over for 30 minutes
